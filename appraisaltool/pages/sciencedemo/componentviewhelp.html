What does "Component" mean?

A component is one constituent part of a complex digital object. For example, in digital video artworks, the media player is regarded as a component.

Explanations of each column in the "Components" table

Name: the name of the component.

Type: the type of the component.

Used in % of all Objects: The usage level of the component, represented in percentage.

Risk Type: primary risk of the object.

Date for action: the date action is needed, predicted by the risk impact algorithm.

Confidence: confidence interval of the prediction
